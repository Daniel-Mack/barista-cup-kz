About the Event

The first-ever barista competition in Kazakhstan! A platform for coffee professionals to showcase their skills, exchange knowledge, and celebrate coffee culture.

Competition Stages

  • Coffee Quiz: Up to 100 participants, top 24 advance.
  • Speed & Latte Art Battle: 24 competitors, top 12 finalists.
  • Final Presentation & Tasting: Brew and present espresso, cappuccino, flat white, and alternative milk drinks.

Special Activities

Free masterclasses, networking events, and a grand award ceremony await participants and guests!
